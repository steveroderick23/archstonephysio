Orthopedic Injuries

At Archstone Physiotherapy and Wellness Centre you can expect your injuries to be assessed in a completely integrated way. We use a whole body approach, taking into account the way your entire body works.

Our focus is on getting you back to moving well with strength and confidence whether you have a new acute injury or are struggling with a persistent or recurring problem. We will work with you one on one, with minimal emphasis on passive treatments such as heat, ice and TENS machines. While these can certainly play a role in promoting healing, our philosophy is that these should only be an adjunct to more hands on, active rehabilitation. We will help you shift your focus from pain management to movement freedom, moving the way your body was designed.
