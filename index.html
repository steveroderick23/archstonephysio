We offer care in the following primary areas

Pelvic And Abdominal Dysfunction For Women And Men

Problems with your pelvic floor can have a significant impact on your quality of life, with difficulties ranging from incontinence, low back, pelvic, or hip pain, to sexual difficulties. Click here for more information on how physiotherapy can help women and men who are having problems with the pelvic floor.

Pelvic And Abdominal Dysfunction For Children


Non invasive physiotherapy techniques are highly effective for pediatric pelvic floor disorders such as bedwetting, daytime wetting, bowel accidents, and constipation. Click to find out more about pediatric pelvic floor treatments with Patty Steele, PT

Hand And Upper Extremity Rehabilitation


If you have an injury or pain from a condition that affects your upper extremities—your hand, wrist, elbow, or shoulder, you may benefit from seeing Sue Kean, our Certified Hand Physiotherapist.




Pregnancy And Healthy Recovery From Childbirth

Bringing a new baby into the world is a wonderful, life changing experience, but the tremendous changes your body goes through can sometimes leave you feeling a little beat up. At Archstone we want to equip you with the tools to help you have a positive pregnancy and birth experience, and return to a healthy, active lifestyle. To find out more about how we can work with you through this exciting time, please click here.

Orthopedic Injuries


Our main focus is to help you move and function better. We use a whole body assessment and treatment approach to get to the root of the problem. If an injury or pain is impacting your ability to move with strength and confidence, click here to find out more about our treatment philosophy and approach.

Massage And Acupuncture


Massage therapy and acupuncture can be an important part of your health maintenance plan. Click to find out more.

What can you expect at Archstone?

  • Private treatment rooms in a quiet, respectful, and confidential environment. One on one individual treatments that look at your whole body.
  • Child friendly clinic. We recognize that moms come with children and many women come to us following childbirth and pregnancy. It’s a package! If you do not have child care, you are welcome to attend with your child. We will do our best to help you.
  • Physiotherapists have direct access in New Brunswick and do not require a physician referral. However, some insurance companies may require a referral from a physician. A quick call to your insurance carrier can answer any questions you may have.
  • Treatment with up to date technology including pelvic floor EMG biofeedback and neuro muscular stimulation.
  • A strong focus on active treatment strategies to get you back to moving and living with strength and confidence
  • We are able to direct bill most insurance companies.
